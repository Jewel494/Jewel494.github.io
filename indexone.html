This site strives to help cats in need by promoting people to be better and kinder to cats. Our core values here at Save Cats are kindesss, caring, and appreciation for our cats.

Kindness

Kindness is essential everywhere, especially when helping cats. Being kind to a cat by simply feeding the cat or rescuing it can have a tremendous impact on their well-being. At Save Cats, it is our mission to help cats in anyway possible, and that is why we believe in the power of kindness.

Caring

It is our priority to make sure cats are taken care of to the greatest lengths possible. That is why we link to only the best animal rescues and organizations and make sure cats only receive the best treatment. After all, a well-cared cat will be a happy cat.

Appreciation

Appreciating our cats is not something to be forgotten and isn't forgotten by us. Here we love our cats and all appreciate everything that they bring to us, from fun memories to comfort to their very presence.
